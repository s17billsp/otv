About

OTV Status is a fan made project to let fans like myself get all the latest/live content from OfflineTV without having to go through Twitch, YouTube or Twitter individually.

I have not (nor do I plan on) adding ads to this site, no analytics or tracking beyond what twitch/youtube add via their video embeds has been added either.
